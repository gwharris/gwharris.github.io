About Me

Welcome! My name's Graham - I'm a rising junior at NYU majoring in Math and minoring in Computer Science and Italian.

As a student, I spend a lot of time in and out of the classroom working on various projects. When I'm not at school, I enjoy coding, taking care of plants, and trying new food!

Coding: I've been learning how to code for about a year and am always looking for new projects. I have experience in client-side coding and development, and am currently learning web and app development. I pride myself in being able to learn new languages quickly and enjoy picking up new styles of code.

Food: As an NYU student, I have access to NYC's rich food scene, which I take advantage of through Bite Club. We tackle all sorts of topics ranging from lighthearted trends, fads and recipes, to more serious topics, such as racism and sustainability in the NYC food industry. For the 2020-2021 academic year, I'll be the club's president, and I look forward to trying even more local restaurants and bringing my passion for food to my community.

Our mission at Bite Club is to make food topics more accessible to the NYU community and to empower our staff. Bite Club has three main pillars: editorial work, social media, and marketing/PR.

  • Editorial - The editorial team writes about restaurants, recipes, current events in the food industry.
  • Social - On the Bite Club instagram (@biteclubnyu), we post pictures of whatever our staff is eating. We feature our sponsors first and foremost, but also love to display our staff's creations!
  • Marketing - In exchange for product, our team posts to the aforementioned instagram page. We then distribute the product and promotional materials to NYU students through club events.
